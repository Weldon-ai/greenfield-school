About Green-Field

Discover our mission, vision, and commitment to excellence.

Green-Field School was founded in 1995 with the vision of providing holistic education that nurtures both academic excellence and character development. Located in the serene environment of Sergoit, Iten, we offer a conducive learning atmosphere where students thrive.

Our curriculum integrates academics, sports, arts, and technology, ensuring that every student discovers and develops their unique talents. We are committed to shaping responsible, creative, and skilled future leaders who will make a positive impact on society.

With modern facilities, dedicated teachers, and a supportive community, Green-Field School stands as a beacon of quality education in Elgeyo Marakwet County and beyond.
